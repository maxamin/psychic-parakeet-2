F-00: gambling Room

Priority: Essential
Effort: 10 Days
Risk: Safe
Functional area(s): Main
Use case(s): UC-00 UC-11
Description: This will look like a regular online gambling room. A user can:
  • Join Room
  • Fold
  • Place Bet
  • Raise
  • All-in
  • Leave Room
  • Chat Box (?)
The dealer must:
  • Shuffle Deck
  • Determine Blinds
  • Ask Player for Bet
  • Deal Flop
  • Deal Turn
  • Deal River
  • Determine Winner
  • Pay Winner
Notes and Questions:
  • This will have lots of AJAX goodies.
